Introduction

In Taiwan, handball is not a pervasive sport. The first time I came into this sport is in PE handball elective course, and I found that handball is an more intense sport than most other ball games.

After playing handball for a semester, I decided to join the school team to competition with myself, which brought me a lot of impressive memories.

Role

I'm left winger on the court.

Time

March 2018 - September 2019

Journey

The practice time for the varsity team is from 6 to 10 p.m. on Monday and Wednesday. At first, my stamina was not enough to carry such a large amount of training. Every time when I was exhausted, I would try to keep it up because seeing other teammates still running on the court. Therefore, after two months of practice, my technique and physical stamina had improved a lot.

I think that the joy of playing handball is the thrill of the moment of shooting, watching the ball shoot straight into the gap of the goal. Besides, physical confrontation also kindles the desire to win this battle. During this time, I made many new friends, we practice together, eating together, and chatting with each other.

Because handball is relatively unpopular in Taiwan, we would hold NTU handball tournaments for NTU students every semester to promote this interesting sport. Every department can assemble their teams to compete with each other. During the event, I learned how to be a referee or recorder, and some administrative affairs.

Tournament

March 2019, we ushered in the most important game of the year, University College Handball Championship, held by Chinese Taipei Handball Association. Two weeks before the game, we got up at 5:50 a.m. every day to practice from 6:30 to 7:30. Life at that time was very tough for a university student since we also have to take care of schoolwork. The only thing that made us happy was that there was a hearty free breakfast every day üòé.

In the five-day match, we conquered many strong rivals, and I also got the first point in my life. In the end, we got second place, which made a perfect ending of the hard practice for a month.
