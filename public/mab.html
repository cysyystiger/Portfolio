Introduction

Multi-Armed Bandit is an interesting problem in probability theory, if you are not familiar with the problem formulation, you can read this first.

We put emphasis on upper bound regrets for some algorithms, and we would show some simulations to compare the outcomes of mathematical deduction. Also, the multi-armed bandit problems with delay or anonymous rewards are inclusive. Generally speaking, we would write regret formulas in terms of times and the number of arms to observe algorithms incisively.

Role

I would report my process and discuss it with the professor once two weeks, and the professor would give me some suggestions and methods or propose some questions unsolved. During the period, I read many papers and try to figure out how delay and anonymous property influence the upper bound of regret.
