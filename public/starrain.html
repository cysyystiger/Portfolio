Introduction

Starrain is a student service club at National Taiwan University (NTU). Our service targets are children and teenagers with autism. We would organize educational activities four times a semester, which makes them stimulated by the special environment different from their daily lives. Also, we try hard to let them get some new experience, technique, and an appropriate attitude towards society.

"Understanding autistic children, stimulating autistic children, and making autistic children grow up" is our consistent expectation for this service. Because of congenital defects, the autistic has obstacles in perception, movement, emotion, behavior, etc. Therefore, they can't study normally like ordinary children. Moreover, due to the ignorance of the general public, the failure to give autistic children an appropriate and proper educational environment and resources has caused the function of autistic children to degenerate and become a burden on the family, school, and society.

We hope that through the establishment of the NTU Starrain, we will guide more people to understand autism services and organize activities for autistic children in a constructed way to increase their ability to adapt to society. Based on this, the proposed objectives are as follows:

  1. Increase group membersâ€™ awareness and recognition of autism services.
  2. Provide stimulation through activities to increase the ability of autistic children to adapt to society.
  3. Play a supportive role in the life of autistic children.
  4. The members learn and grow through planned and continuous service.

The origin of the name "Starrain" can be divided into two parts. "Star" comes from their characteristic. The main characteristics of autism are indifference to emotions, refusal to communicate, delayed language development, stereotyped behavior repetition, and significant limitations in the scope of activities. Therefore, people are accustomed to calling autistic patients "children of the stars." They live in their own world and twinkle alone. "Rain" comes from the famous movie, "Rain Man", depicting a story of an autistic and his brother in Las Vegas.

Activity

We would have unambiguous objectives for a semester. For example, the below are objectives for 108-1 semester.

Objective

Use the elements generated from the experience activities to encourage children to express themselves

Experience activities

Definition: Things that they rarely do

Purpose: Let children have something to express

Express

Definition: According to the standard form given by the activity

Level: 1. Do not look at the elements given by the activity to express

2. Look at the elements to express themselves

3. Express through our guidance

Our position is to let them delineate their experiences and express emotions. The reason we want to build this objective is that expression itself is the skills they are not adept at. At the same time, we expect to have new stimulations and interesting activities to make them happy and more willing to express themselves.
